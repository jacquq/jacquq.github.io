Quentin Jacquet

INRIA, CMAP, Ecole Polytechnique, EDF R&D, Saclay (CIFRE PhD) quentin.jacquet@edf.fr

I am a second year PhD student at INRIA, at CMAP, Ecole Polytechnique, and at EDF R&D, Saclay (CIFRE PhD). I am supervised by Stéphane Gaubert (INRIA, CMAP), Wim Van Ackooij (EDF) and Clémence Alasseur (EDF).

My research is about optimization and applications to electricity pricing.
